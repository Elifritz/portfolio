Welcome to my web portolio!

My name is Trevor Elifritz. I was born and raised in Madison, Wisconsin. After graduating high school, I worked a plethora of odd jobs in Madison and Tallahassee, Florida with my now fiancee. A combination of motivation from my life partner and the struggles of low-income life drove me to seek something more fulfilling. In that pursuit, I graduated from UW-Madison with majors in Cartography/GIS and History.

My primary passions are in GIS analysis, spatial statistics and historical research. A more particular interest of mine is the utilization of techniques from both the fields of GIS and History to solve practical problems holistically.

In this web portolio you will find examples of my work both from college and personal pursuits. Enjoy your stay and thanks for visiting.
